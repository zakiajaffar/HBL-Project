What is Konnect?

Konnect is a branchless banking solution that empowers customers to fulfill their banking and payment needs in the most convenient and secure way. Customers can avail Konnect services by visiting the nearest Konnect Shop or through the Mobile phone using Konnect account.

What is a Konnect Account?

Konnect account is a branchless banking account that is tagged with your mobile number and can be operated through your mobile phone. It is an easy to use bank account, which enables customer to pay bills, transfer money, make retail payments, recharge mobile credit etc. at their convenience.

What is my Konnect Account Number?

Your mobile number is your Konnect Account number..

What is the difference between Level 0 and Level 1 account?

These two accounts are only different in terms of daily/monthly/annual transaction and account balance limits.

Who can be a Konnect Accountholder?

Any person holding a valid CNIC and a PTA registered mobile number can be a Konnect Account holder. You will be required to registered for Konnect account using mobile number registered in your name at PTA.

Can I open more than one Konnect Account on a single CNIC?

No, you can only open one Konnect Account per CNIC.

Can I transfer the ownership of my Konnect Account to someone else?

No, you are not allowed to transfer ownership of your Konnect Account. If you are no longer using it, you need to request closure of your Konnect Account..

How to open a Konnect Account?

You can open your Konnect account through a multiple set of channels including:

Konnect Mobile App

a. Level 0 Account via Konnect Mobile App.

  • Step 1: Download the Konnect Mobile app through Play store/App Store
  • Step 2: Click on ‘SIGN UP’
  • Step 3: Provide all required details
  • Step 4: Set your Login ID, Password and MPIN
  • Step 5: Upon successful registration and KYC completion your account will be activated and you will receive an SMS notification from 8425 on your registered mobile number.
    • SMS

    b. Level 0 Account via SMS

  • Step 1: Send the following SMS to 8425
      BBAO< SPACE > < CNIC > < SPACE > < CITY >
      Example: BBAO 4230190039220 Karachi
  • Step 2: Read the T&Cs thoroughly received through the link
  • Step 3: You will receive a call from Konnect Customer Care representative for verification of your details
  • Step 4: Upon successful verification and completion of KYC your account will be activated and you will receive an account activation SMS from 8425
    • Konnect Shops (Agents)

    c. Level 1 Account via Konnect Shop

  • Step 1: Visit your nearest Konnect Shop. click here to locate your nearest agent.
  • Step 2: Provide your Name, CNIC Number and biometric impression along with other required details.
  • Step 3: Upon successful verification and completion of KYC, your account will be activated and you will receive an SMS alert from 8425 on your registered mobile number.
    • HBL Biometric ATM

    d. Level 1 Account via HBL Biometric ATM

  • Step 1: Visit the nearest HBL Biometric ATM.
  • Step 2: Select your preferred language.
  • Step 3: Select ‘Register Branchless Banking Account’.
  • Step 4: Provide your Name, CNIC number and biometric impression along with other required details.
  • Step 5: Upon successful verification and completion of KYC, your account will be activated and you will receive an SMS alert on your registered mobile number
    • What are the transactions limits for Konnect Accounts?

    Depending on the account type, the following transaction limits are available for Konnect Account holders:

    Limits Level 0 Account PKR) Level 1 Account PKR)
    Daily Transaction 25,000 50,000
    Monthly Transaction 40,000 80,000
    Account Balance 200,000 800,000
    These Limites are set on the cumulative mobile account transactions (via any available channel)
    Can I request for limit enhancement for my Konnect Account?

    Yes, if you have a Level 0 account you can enhance your transaction and balance limit by upgrading your account to Level 1. You simply need to visit your nearest Konnect Shop or nearest HBL Biometric ATM for account upgrade.

    Are there any annual fees for using Konnect Account?

    There is no annual fee or charges for opening a Konnect Account. However, please note that transactions made are subject to transaction charges in accordance with HBL’s Schedule of Bank Charges (SOBC), which can be viewed on the following link: www.hbl.com/konect.

    Can I use the existing HBL Internet Banking or HBL Mobile User ID and Password or logging in to Konnect app?

    No, HBL Internet Banking credentials cannot be used on Konnect mobile application. A new Username and Password needs to be created for Konnect Mobile app by tapping the register button on Konnect Mobile app.

    What if, I forget my login Password?

    You can recover your password using OTP method described below:

    Change Password Using OTP

  • Open your Konnect Mobile app
  • Select Forgot Password or Username Option
  • Select ‘Change Password using OTP’
  • Enter your CNIC Number and Date of Birth
  • OTP for password reset will be sent on your registered mobile number
  • Enter OTP and upon successful verification, app will redirect you to set new password
  • Follow instructions and set a new password
  • Once new password is set, SMS notification will be sent on your registered mobile number.
    • What if I change my regular device cell phone, can I use Konnect Mobile App with the same login and password?

    Yes, you can login with the same user ID password on your new cell phone device

    What should I do in case my cellphone gets lost/stolen?

    Get you Konnect Account blocked immediately through calling on Konnect Customer Care 111-425-111. As a precaution you may also, login to Konnect Mobile app from another device and change your password and TPIN immediately.

    If I wish to block my account on temporary basis, can I do it through Konnect Mobile application?

    You need to call Konnect Customer Care at 021-111-111-425 to block your Konnect Mobile account temporarily. In order to unblock your account, you may again call Konnect Customer Care on 111-425-111

    How do I register Funds Transfer beneficiaries?

    Go to Add Beneficiary section under beneficiary management and provide details of beneficiary. A One Time Password (OTP) will be sent to your mobile number registered with HBL as a validation for adding beneficiary. Enter OTP and upon successful validation, beneficiary will be added.

    How do I edit or delete funds transfer beneficiaries?

    You can manage your Beneficiaries by using Add or Edit Beneficiaries option under Fund Transfer section.
